# Competitor Screenshot Inspiration Board

Real external screenshots were not copied into the repo to avoid licensing ambiguity. Instead, this board records visual patterns observed from public App Store/site previews and web references.

## Widgetsmith inspiration
- Large widget preview as the hero object.
- Size/category-driven creation path.
- Personalization language: “make your phone yours.”

## Widgy inspiration
- Power editor depth.
- Layer/template model.
- Strong live preview feedback.

## Color Widgets inspiration
- Gallery-first browsing.
- Theme systems that combine multiple surface types.
- Fast “choose and apply” flow.

## WidgetClub inspiration
- Template abundance.
- Aesthetic packs for beginners.
- Clear visual cards with style identity.

## StudyPlanner rule
Adapt patterns, do not copy layouts, claims, screenshots, iconography, or paid positioning. StudyPlanner’s differentiator is schoolwork utility + real planner-backed widgets, not generic phone decoration.
